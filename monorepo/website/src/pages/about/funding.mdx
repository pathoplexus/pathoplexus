To date, Pathoplexus has largely been built with donated resources and time. We are actively seeking funding to drive the platform forward. We are a registered non-profit in Switzerland; if you would like a donation receipt for your taxes, please reach out to donations@pathoplexus.org. Donations do not affect governance or operational decisions.

## Support Pathoplexus

We welcome support from both institutions and individuals. For institutional partnerships and major funding opportunities, please contact us at hello@pathoplexus.org to discuss how you could support Pathoplexus and what we could achieve together.  We also welcome donations from individuals. We can accept donations in a number of ways: 

**Donate via Stripe** - you can donate via Stripe using credit/debit cards, Apple Pay or Google Pay.
