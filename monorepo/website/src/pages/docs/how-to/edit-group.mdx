---

Any member of a group can edit group information, which means that you can edit group information for all the groups that you are a member of.

1. Log into your account and enter your account page by clicking 'My account' in the top-right corner of the webpage
2. Under 'My groups' click the group that you want to edit
3. In the group detail view, click the 'Edit group' button in the top right corner
4. Edit any property of the group you want to change and click 'Update group' to save your changes
