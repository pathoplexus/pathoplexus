Pathoplexus is a powerful platform designed to streamline the process of managing, sharing, and analyzing pathogen sequence data. This documentation serves as a guide to understanding and utilizing the features of Pathoplexus effectively.

## How to use this documentation

Our documentation is divided into two main sections:

1. **Concepts**: Dive deep into the terms and formats used in Pathoplexus. This section explains key terms and processes that make up the platform.

2. **How to**: Step-by-step guides for common tasks and operations within Pathoplexus. Whether you're setting up your account, uploading sequences, or using advanced features, you'll find detailed instructions here.

## Getting started

New to Pathoplexus? Begin your journey by:

1. [Creating an account](/docs/how-to/create-account)
2. [Linking your ORCiD](/docs/how-to/add-orcid-account)

If you have already run into some trouble accessing or using some of our features, chances are high that someone else has ever faced a similar challenge. Please check out our [FAQs](https://pathoplexus.org/about/faq) page for any matches to your issue. In case you are unable to find a solution both in the FAQs and documentation, or identified issues not covered in this documentation, please don't hesitate to contact our support team or report issues directly through the platform.
